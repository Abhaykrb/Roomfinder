1. First signin by entering your details like username,email etc
  2. Use that signedin username and password to login
  3. After login you can view the available rooms on a map by clicking Viewmap link
  4. By clicking available markers you can see the details about rooms
  5. You can also put available rooms on map by using '/admin' in home page where you will be see options to add roomws
  6. Finally logout by clicking logout
